# AtliQ Hardwares – SQL Ad Hoc Business Insights

## 📌 Project Overview
A SQL-based exploration of AtliQ Hardwares' sales and product data to uncover 
regional market trends, portfolio growth, channel performance, and pricing 
dynamics driving strategic business decisions.

---

## 🏢 Company Background
AtliQ Hardwares is one of India's leading computer hardware producers with a 
strong and growing global presence. The management identified a critical gap — 
limited data visibility for fast, informed decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To solve 10 real-world ad hoc business requests using advanced MySQL queries 
and present data-driven insights to top-level management through a structured 
visual presentation.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Database:** MySQL
- **Presentation:** Microsoft PowerPoint
- **Skills:** SQL Querying, Data Analysis, Business Insights, Data Visualization

---

## 💡 SQL Concepts Used
- SELECT, WHERE, GROUP BY, HAVING, ORDER BY, LIMIT
- JOINs (INNER, LEFT)
- CTEs (WITH clause)
- Window Functions (RANK, DENSE_RANK)
- CASE Statements
- Subqueries
- Aggregate Functions (COUNT, AVG, SUM, MIN, MAX)

## 📊 Business Requests & Key Findings

| # | Business Request | Key Finding |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Markets where Atliq Exclusive operates in APAC | 8 markets — India, Japan, Australia & more |
| 2 | Unique product % increase 2021 vs 2020 | 36.33% growth |
| 3 | Unique product count by segment | Notebook leads with 129 products |
| 4 | Segment with highest product growth | Accessories — +34 new products |
| 5 | Highest & lowest manufacturing cost products | ₹240.54 vs ₹0.89 |
| 6 | Top 5 customers by discount % in India FY2021 | Flipkart — 30.83% |
| 7 | Monthly gross revenue for Atliq Exclusive | November peaks — festive season demand |
| 8 | Quarter with max sold quantity in 2020 | Q1 — 13.58M units |
| 9 | Channel contribution to FY2021 gross sales | Retailer — 73.23% |
| 10 | Top 3 products per division FY2021 | N&S leads with Pen Drive products |

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- Atliq Exclusive operates across **8 APAC markets** — a high-value regional partner
- Product portfolio grew **36%** in one year, driven by the Accessories segment
- **November** consistently peaks in sales due to Diwali & festive season demand
- Summer months **(Apr–Jun)** consistently underperform
- **Retailer channel** contributes nearly 3/4 of total FY2021 gross sales
- **Q1 2020** was the strongest quarter (13.58M units); Q2–Q3 dip reflects COVID-19 impact
- Manufacturing cost ranges from **₹0.89 to ₹240.54** — wide product spectrum
